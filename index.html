Brief Setting Summary

The Ethos of Mirelle is a daily part of life in the region and none can remember a time before every village, town, and city had access to a temple and helpful priests available. The past few human generations have been noteworthy due to an industrial revolution led by the Goddess’s servants. Advances in medicine, engineering, and transportation have brought significant quality of life improvements in the cities and while the small towns still lag behind, it’s only a matter of time before this progress and these technologies reach them too.

The Ethos of Mirelle

The Ethos is the major central religion in the setting and revolves around worship of the Goddess Mirelle. The tenets are standard lawful good religious fare: don’t kill, don’t steal, protect those less fortunate, etc. and the only known proscription is against necromancy spells. They have the best stuff. (If anyone plays a Cleric or Paladin in the service of Mirelle, they will be provided more details.) It’s my default assumption that anyone playing a Cleric or Paladin is part of the Ethos, so if anyone wants that not to be the case, we’ll work together to hash out the details.
